Spring 2017 Recruitment

Wednesday, February 15 - Friday February 17

What is it?

Recruitment week is an opportunity for students to learn about Alpha Phi Omega and become acquainted with active members.

Who can join?

Any Stanford student can rush Alpha Phi Omega. We welcome students of all genders, ethnicities, majors, etc.

Do you haze?

There is no hazing. Alpha Phi Omega has a strict policy against hazing.
